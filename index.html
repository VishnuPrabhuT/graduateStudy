Project Overview

I am interested in making a role playing game. Entire theme of the game would be surrounded around a character played by a Protagonist, who would try to evade and destroy the antagonist to reach a certain goal to complete the game.

Game Logic

I look forward to delve into game development features like Waypoint, Navmesh spatial position triggering along with trying Pathfinding technologies for enemy scripting. Although, I haven't tried AI scripts in any games yet, this project could be a first in trying out new process of game development.
